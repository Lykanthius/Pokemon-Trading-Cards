Pokémon Cards

Blastoise

Blastoise uses its water cannons to protect its family and clean its environment.

  • Hydro Shield: Prevent damage next turn. Heal 30 if Blastoise has Water Energy.
  • Steam Precision: 60 damage, ignoring Resistance and effects.
  • Torrent Cannon: 120 damage to the Active Pokémon, 30 to a Benched Pokémon.
  • Guardian Geyser: Heal 50 for Blastoise and Benched Water Pokémon. Draw 1 card each.

Blastoise views its trainer as part of its "shell."

Charizard

The temperature and color of Charizard’s flames reflect its emotional state.

  • Infernal Bond: 30 damage per Fire Energy. Attach 1 Energy if a Pokémon is knocked out.
  • Nest Embers: Flip a coin. Heads: 100 damage. Tails: Attach 2 Fire Energy.
  • Molten Wing:: Discard 1 Energy. Deal 150 damage.
  • Flare of Resolve: Attach up to 3 Fire Energy. Take 20 damage.

Once forged, the bond of a Charizard is unbreakable.

Mewtwo

Mewtwo struggles with its origin, but senses the emotions of others through telepathy.

  • Eclipse of Purpose: 120 damage with Mew. Without Mew, deal 80 and attach 1 Energy.
  • Empathic Surge: 20 damage for each Pokémon with damage. Heal 20 for each of yours.
  • Genesis Rift: Deal 200 damage, discard 2 Energy. With Mew, shuffle 2 discarded Energy instead.
  • Miraculous Duo: Use attacks of your Benched or discarded Pokémon.

Mewtwo finds purpose through its connection to Mew.
